## Transcript Summary

Ross discusses why content distribution is often more important than content creation. He explains how marketers can extend the life of existing content through repurposing and strategic distribution.

## Key Takeaways

1. Great content needs distribution to create business impact.
2. Repurposing increases ROI on content investments.
3. Distribution should be planned before content is published.

## Why It Matters for LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy

LinkedIn success depends not only on creating content but also on maximizing visibility through distribution and repurposing.
